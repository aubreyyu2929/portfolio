My Design Concept

Title: Don't be in the dark

Purpose: It is a web-based edutainment which can teach teenagers the history of Australia mineral and safety instruction of mine.

Audience

Primary Audience: 14-18 years old, Australian residents who are interested in the mineral industry or history.

Secondary Audience: Teachers/ headmaster/kids’ parent who wants to know the detail of the website and will recommend primary audience to use it.

interactivity

  • I use the interactive map and let the user collect the mineral resources which just like the fun of collecting cards.
  • Gestural interaction by stretching and moving map. During the process, the audience can explore Australia Map, and in this process, they can learn the history of Australia Mineral industry typical 2-finger pinch gesture to zoom in and out
  • Otherwise, they can also post a question on the Forum, and students or others can reply to the question.
  • The dataset can also be improved by others who can add the information which is not complete.

    • SLQ database

    Dataset: State Library of Queensland - Queensland mining accidents 1882-1945

    Other Datasets: Queensland mining and exploration tenure series

    Target Audience

    Primary Audience: Our main target audience is white-collar workers and students who like to read. For workers, a library is a significant place. Based on the fierce competition in employment nowadays, workers also need to continue to learn in their free time in order to maintain their high employment value. Secondly, unlike students, students can enjoy the preferential treatment and quiet environments of the school library, such as free entry and free lending. It is difficult for workers to find a suitable reading place during work or free time. At this moment, the emergence of our website is meaningful. In addition, the website also provides event browsing. This means that users can not only read at the reading spot but also participate in some professional or non-professional activities to know more like-minded friends. For students, the meaning of the website is to facilitate the group of students who do not often go to school but enjoy the library atmosphere. As far as I am concerned, the location is closer to the State Library, and I prefer to go there instead of the uni library during the free time of the weekend. Then, off-campus reading spots can also make students contact with more friends besides students. Not only can they expand the scope of students' knowledge, but it can also bring them a happy experience.

    Secondary Audience: The definition of a website is for those people who are looking for a suitable reading spot, so anyone with this need can be our potential target audience. For example, parents can take their children to the reading point to accompany the learning process. Older people can go to the library to participate in some exciting social activities. Professionals can go to the library to access materials and participate in lectures. In short, as long as they are people who like to read, our website can provide them with different functions.

    Purpose

    The purpose for us to design this website is to help the target audience quickly and accurately find the suitable place to learn or work. Traditionally, people may think that the library is the only place to study and read books. However, bookstores and some cafes are also good choices for quiet reading. On this website, we have integrated libraries, bookstores, and cafes as suitable choices for reading points, showing a list of results to choose from based on the user's search filter.

    Group Work Reflection

    I think teamwork has two side. One side is its benefit, and the other one is its challenge. In a team, I can share my workload for the entire team, which can increase work productivity and the quality if the team members work well together. The challenge of teamwork includes conflicts and balance of workload. If the leader didn’t keep the balance of workload between team members, the people who did little would feel left out sometime, and people who did a lot would feel unbalanced. Besides, trust together, design roles clearly and esteem are also important factor.

    I feel honoured that I can be the leader in our team, which give me a chance to anneal my organization and management skill Then I will discuss my feeling of this group work in communication, work management, meeting, conflict, and misconduct below.

    Communication: In the process I communicate with my team members by WeChat and face-to-face talk. I think face-to-face talk is more efficiently than send massage online. Thus, sometimes I will arrange two meeting in one week. One meeting is for work allocation, and the other one is for us to work together can talk about the difficulties we face individually. I feel as a team leader I should listen my team members advices in the project, which I did well. But I didn’t push my team members to finish one goal sometimes, because I am not this kind of guy. I afraid that I over push my team members to work on something and cause dissatisfaction.

    Work Management: In most cases, I will list all the thing we should finish this week and let team member choose which one they want to do.In this process, I found a problem that a team member Runan CHEN is busy with her study, and she always has excuse such as she can’t do this because she busy with other team work.
